About Us

The Interdisciplinary Development Research Forum (IDRF) Established in 2024 with the moto of societal reforms with research-oriented development activities which includes all dimensions like through research, capacity building, human resource development, knowledge hubs. Our core mission revolves around disseminating knowledge and providing targeted training to enhance national capacity. IDRF actively engages in diverse activities, including conducting research projects, facilitating awareness programs, implementing skill development initiatives, fostering human resource capacity building, conducting evidence-based research, orchestrating intervention programs, and contributing to the development of Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) projects. At the heart of our mission lies a dedication to making a tangible impact on society through holistic and strategic endeavors.
